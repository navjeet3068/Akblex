Publications & Insights

Books

Dr. Bansal has authored and revised several authoritative works on arbitration and dispute resolution law.

Arbitration Awards (Three Editions)
Universal Law Publishing Pvt. Ltd.
First book exclusively dealing with arbitration agreements and awards.
International Commercial Arbitration: Enforcement of Foreign Awards in More Than 75 Countries
Universal Law Publishing Pvt. Ltd.
A comprehensive work on enforcement of arbitral awards under the New York Convention. Focuses on practice, procedure, and country-specific application of the Convention.
Commentary on Arbitration (Two Volumes)
Bharat Law House, New Delhi
Section-wise commentary on the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, as interpreted by various courts in India.
Arbitration and ADR (2005)
Universal Law Publishing Pvt. Ltd.
A textbook on alternative dispute resolution.
Arbitration & ADR (Universal Law Series)
Universal Law Publishing Co.
An essential revision aid for law students, designed as a comprehensive study and reference guide for arbitration and alternative dispute resolution.
Material and Case Law under the New Arbitration Act (1999)
Arbitration Law Reporter
A compilation of materials and case law under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. (Out of print)
Arbitration & ADR (Universal's Legal Learning Text)
Universal Law Publishing Co.
A systematic legal learning text on arbitration, conciliation, mediation, negotiation, expert determination, and alternative dispute resolution systems in India.
International Commercial Arbitration: A Study of Jurisdiction by Indian Courts (Doctoral Thesis)
Department of Law, Punjabi University, Patiala
Dr. Bansal's doctoral thesis presented in 2012, examining judicial intervention and the scope of jurisdiction exercised by Indian courts in foreign arbitrations.
